# Google Ads vs SEO: Which Is Better for Small Businesses?

## Introduction

Businesses often struggle to choose between SEO and Google Ads. Both can generate leads, but they work differently.

## What is SEO?

SEO improves organic rankings on search engines.

### Advantages

- Long-term traffic
- Higher credibility
- Sustainable results

### Disadvantages

- Takes time
- Requires ongoing optimization

## What are Google Ads?

Google Ads are paid advertisements displayed in search results.

### Advantages

- Immediate visibility
- Fast lead generation
- Flexible budget control

### Disadvantages

- Stops when spending stops
- Can become expensive

## Which Option is Better?

For immediate results:
Google Ads

For long-term growth:
SEO

For best performance:
Use both strategies together.

## Conclusion

Small businesses can benefit from combining SEO and Google Ads to maximize visibility and conversions.
